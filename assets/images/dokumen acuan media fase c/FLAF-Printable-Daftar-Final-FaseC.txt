LEGENDA
=======
FC   = Flash card kosakata (1254×1254 px, 1:1)
WS   = Worksheet / lembar isian (1054×1492 px, A4)
REF  = Reference card / panduan (1054×1492 px, A4)
TEXT = Teks cetak siap baca (1054×1492 px, A4)
SORT = Sorting/game card — dipotong dan dimanipulasi (1254×1254 px, 1:1)
POST = Poster / project template (1054×1492 px, A4)

Catatan SORT: tp-c14-juice-step*.png dicetak 1 set per pasangan,
dipotong menjadi 5 kartu terpisah, diurutkan siswa.


CATATAN BRIEF ILUSTRATOR
=========================
C12 TEXT — menu-card-a & menu-card-b:
  Format kartu teks fungsional bersih (bukan ilustrasi bergambar)
  Menu Card A: No · Food · Price · What is it like?
    1. Nasi Goreng   Rp 15.000   spicy
    2. Mie Goreng    Rp 12.000   not spicy
    3. Ayam Goreng   Rp 18.000   crispy
  Menu Card B:
    4. Nasi Uduk   Rp 12.000   savory
    5. Bakso       Rp 10.000   spicy
    6. Es Teh      Rp  5.000   sweet and cold
  Ilustrasi dekoratif kecil di header (warung Indonesia)

C14 SORT — juice-step1 s/d step5:
  Dicetak 1 set per pasangan → dipotong 5 kartu
  Setiap kartu: ilustrasi 1 langkah membuat jus jeruk
  Nomor urut TIDAK dicetak — siswa yang mengurutkan
  Garis potong jelas antar kartu
  Kanvas: 1254×1254 px per kartu

C15 TEXT — story-card-a & story-card-b:
  Story Card A: "One day, Budi went to the market with his mother.
    He saw his friend Sari there. Sari carried a big bag of vegetables."
  Story Card B: "Budi helped Sari carry the bag.
    They walked home together. They felt happy and tired."
  Format: teks besar terbaca, ilustrasi kecil di samping

C18 REF — past-tense-ref:
  Dua kolom: Today | Yesterday
  go   → went
  buy  → bought
  eat  → ate
  see  → saw
  meet → met
